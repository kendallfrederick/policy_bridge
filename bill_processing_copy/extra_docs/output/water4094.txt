=== OFFICIAL TITLE ===
To amend the Central Utah Project Completion Act to authorize expenditures for the conduct of certain water conservation measures in the Great Salt Lake basin, and for other purposes.

=== STRUCTURED BODY ===
    1.
    Header: Short title
    This Act may be cited as the
        Great Salt Lake Stewardship Act
    .
    2.
    Header: Water conservation measures in the Great Salt Lake basin
    Title II of the Central Utah Project Completion Act (
      Public Law 102â€“575
    ; 106 Stat. 4605) is amended by adding at the end the following:
        213.
        Header: Water conservation measures in the Great Salt Lake basin
          (a)
          Header: In general
          The Secretary may use any unexpended budget authority provided under subsections (a)(2) and (c) of section 202 for the conduct of water conservation measures within the Great Salt Lake basin in accordance with section 207.
          (b)
          Header: Compliance with Definite Plan Report
          Any water conservation measure conducted under subsection (a) shall be considered to be compliant with the Definite Plan Report completed under section 205(a).
