=== OFFICIAL TITLE ===
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize the State offices of rural health program.

=== STRUCTURED BODY ===
    1.
    Header: Short title
    This Act may be cited as the
        State Offices of Rural Health Program Reauthorization Act of 2022
    .
    2.
    Header: State offices of rural health
    Section 338J(i)(1) of the Public Health Service Act (
      42 U.S.C. 254r(i)(1)
    ) is amended by striking
      fiscal years 2018 through 2022
    and inserting
      fiscal years 2023 through 2027
    .
