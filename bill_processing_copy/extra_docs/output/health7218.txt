=== OFFICIAL TITLE ===
To amend title III of the Public Health Service Act to extend the program for promotion of public health knowledge and awareness of Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias, and for other purposes.

=== STRUCTURED BODY ===
    1.
    Header: Short title
    This Act may be cited as the
        BOLD Infrastructure for Alzheimer's Reauthorization Act of 2024
    .
    2.
    Header: Extension of program for promotion of public health knowledge and awareness of Alzheimer’s disease and related dementias
    Section 398B(e) of the Public Health Service Act (
      42 U.S.C. 280c–5(e)
    ) is amended by inserting
      and $33,000,000 for each of fiscal years 2025 through 2029
    before the period at the end.
