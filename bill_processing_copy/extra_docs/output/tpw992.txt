=== OFFICIAL TITLE ===
To amend the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 to designate the Texas and New Mexico portions of the future Interstate-designated segments of the Port-to-Plains Corridor as Interstate Route 27, and for other purposes.

=== STRUCTURED BODY ===
    1.
    Header: Short title
    This Act may be cited as the
        I–27 Numbering Act of 2023
    .
    2.
    Header: Numbering of designated future Interstate
      (a)
      Header: In general
      Section 1105(e)(5)(C)(i) of the Intermodal Surface Transportation Efficiency Act of 1991 (
        Public Law 102–240
      ) is amended by inserting
        The routes referred to in clause (i) (other than subclauses (V)(aa) and (V)(bb) and subclause (IX)(aa) of that clause) and clause (iv) of subsection (c)(38)(A) are designated as Interstate Route I–27. The route referred to in subsection (c)(38)(A)(i)(V)(aa) is designated as Interstate Route I–27E. The route referred to in subsection (c)(38)(A)(i)(V)(bb) is designated as Interstate Route I–27W. The route referred to in subsection (c)(38)(A)(i)(IX)(aa) is designated as Interstate Route I–127N.
      before
        The route referred to in subsection (c)(45)
