

(40503.) Energy auditor training grant program:

(B) (B) The Association of Energy Engineers Certified Energy Auditor certification. (C) (C) The Building Performance Institute Home Energy Professional Energy Auditor certification. (D) (D) The Residential Energy Services Network Home Energy Rater certification. (E) (E) Any other third-party certification recognized by the Department. (F) (F) Any third-party certification that the Secretary determines is equivalent to the certifications described in subparagraphs (A) through (E).
