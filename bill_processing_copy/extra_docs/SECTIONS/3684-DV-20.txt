

(40555.) Rebates: 

(1) (1) $10,000,000 for the extended product system rebate program authorized under section 1005 of the Energy Act of 2020 ( 42 U.S.C. 6311 note; Public Law 116–260 ); and.(2) (2) $10,000,000 for the energy efficient transformer rebate program authorized under section 1006 of the Energy Act of 2020 ( 42 U.S.C. 6317 note; Public Law 116–260 )..
