=== OFFICIAL TITLE ===
To modify the age requirement for the Student Incentive Payment Program of the State maritime academies.

=== STRUCTURED BODY ===
    1.
    Header: Short title
    This Act may be cited as the
        Changing Age-Determined Eligibility To Student Incentive Payments Act
    or the
        CADETS Act
    .
    2.
    Header: Age requirement for the Student Incentive Payment Program of the State maritime academies
    Section 51509 of title 46, United States Code, is amended by adding at the end the following:
        (i)
        Header: Age requirement
        The Secretary may make an agreement under this section only with a qualified student who will meet the age requirement for enlistment or commission in the Navy Reserve at the time of graduation from the academy.
      .
