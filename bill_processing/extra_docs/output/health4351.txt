=== OFFICIAL TITLE ===
To amend the Public Health Service Act to reauthorize certain poison control programs.

=== STRUCTURED BODY ===
    1.
    Header: Short title
    This Act may be cited as the
        Poison Control Centers Reauthorization Act of 2024
    .
    2.
    Header: Reauthorization of poison control programs
      (a)
      Header: National toll-Free number and other communication capabilities
      Section 1271(c) of the Public Health Service Act (
        42 U.S.C. 300d–71(c)
      ) is amended by striking
        fiscal years 2020 through 2024
      and inserting
        fiscal years 2025 through 2029
      .
      (b)
      Header: Promoting poison control center utilization
      Section 1272(c) of the Public Health Service Act (
        42 U.S.C. 300d–72(c)
      ) is amended by striking
        fiscal years 2020 through 2024
      and inserting
        fiscal years 2025 through 2029
      .
      (c)
      Header: Poison control center grant program
      Section 1273(g) of the Public Health Service Act (
        42 U.S.C. 300d–73(g)
      ) is amended by striking
        fiscal years 2020 through 2024
      and inserting
        fiscal years 2025 through 2029
      .
