

(40541.) Grants for energy efficiency improvements and renewable energy improvements at public school facilities: 

(a) (a) Definitions In this section: (1) (1) Alternative fueled vehicle The term alternative fueled vehicle has the meaning given the term in section 301 of the Energy Policy Act of 1992 ( 42 U.S.C. 13211 ). (2) (2) Alternative fueled vehicle infrastructure The term alternative fueled vehicle infrastructure means infrastructure used to charge or fuel an alternative fueled vehicle. (3) (3) Eligible entity The term eligible entity means a consortium of— (A) (A) 1 local educational agency; and (B) (B) 1 or more— (i) (i) schools; (ii) (ii) nonprofit organizations that have the knowledge and capacity to partner and assist with energy improvements; (iii) (iii) for-profit organizations that have the knowledge and capacity to partner and assist with energy improvements; or (iv) (iv) community partners that have the knowledge and capacity to partner and assist with energy improvements. (4) (4) Energy improvement The term energy improvement means— (A) (A) any improvement, repair, or renovation to a school that results in a direct reduction in school energy costs, including improvements to the envelope, air conditioning system, ventilation system, heating system, domestic hot water heating system, compressed air system, distribution system, lighting system, power system, and controls of a building; (B) (B) any improvement, repair, or renovation to, or installation in, a school that— (i) (i) leads to an improvement in teacher and student health, including indoor air quality; and (ii) (ii) achieves energy savings; (C) (C) any improvement, repair, or renovation to a school involving the installation of renewable energy technologies; (D) (D) the installation of alternative fueled vehicle infrastructure on school grounds for— (i) (i) exclusive use of school buses, school fleets, or students; or (ii) (ii) the general public; and (E) (E) the purchase or lease of alternative fueled vehicles to be used by a school, including school buses, fleet vehicles, and other operational vehicles. (5) (5) High school The term high school has the meaning given the term in section 8101 of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 7801 ). (6) (6) Local educational agency The term local educational agency has the meaning given the term in section 8101 of the Elementary and Secondary Education Act of 1965 ( 20 U.S.C. 7801 ).(7) (7) Nonprofit organization The term nonprofit organization means— (A) (A) an organization described in section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986 and exempt from tax under section 501(a) of such Code; or (B) (B) a mutual or cooperative electric company described in section 501(c)(12) of such Code.
