

(50265.) Ensuring energy security: 

(a) (a) Definitions In this section: (1) (1) Federal land The term Federal land means public lands (as defined in section 103 of the Federal Land Policy and Management Act of 1976 ( 43 U.S.C. 1702 )). (2) (2) Offshore lease sale The term offshore lease sale means an oil and gas lease sale— (A) (A) that is held by the Secretary in accordance with the Outer Continental Shelf Lands Act ( 43 U.S.C. 1331 et seq. ); and (B) (B) that, if any acceptable bids have been received for any tract offered in the lease sale, results in the issuance of a lease. (3) (3) Onshore lease sale The term onshore lease sale means a quarterly oil and gas lease sale— (A) (A) that is held by the Secretary in accordance with section 17 of the Mineral Leasing Act ( 30 U.S.C. 226 );
